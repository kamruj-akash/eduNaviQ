About Us

EduNaviQ Technology is an innovative EdTech company focused on providing AI-powered educational guidance and support to students. Our mission is to simplify and optimize the learning journey by combining advanced technology with personalized academic navigation, especially in higher education and skill development.

The name “EduNaviQ” blends “Education”, “Navigation”, and “IQ” (intelligence)—symbolizing our goal to help students make smarter, data-driven decisions about their academic and career paths.

Our platform offers features like smart course suggestions, degree tracking, skill mapping, SOP writing assistance, and intelligent document analysis—powered by artificial intelligence.
