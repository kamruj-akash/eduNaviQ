About EduNaviQ Technology

EduNaviQ Technology is an innovative EdTech company focused on providing AI-powered educational guidance and support to students. Our mission is to simplify and optimize the learning journey by combining advanced technology with personalized academic navigation, especially in higher education and skill development. The name “EduNaviQ” blends “Education”, “Navigation”, and “IQ” (intelligence)—symbolizing our goal to help students make smarter, data-driven decisions about their academic and career paths.
